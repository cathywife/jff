Good options:

http://echarts.baidu.com/
    BSD license.

http://www.highcharts.com
    free for non-commercial, highstock is better than highcharts on time series data
    can export image

http://www.amcharts.com
    free with watermark, excellent interactive UI for time series data
    can export image



https://developers.google.com/chart/
    support zooming but not specially designed for time series data.

http://code.shutterstock.com/rickshaw/
    licensed under MIT, based on d3.js, supports zooming for time series data.
    Designed for streaming data points.

http://www.jqplot.com
    licensed under MIT or GPLv2, interactive UI for time series data is bad, only zooming on rectangular area

http://www.flotcharts.org/
    MIT-like licence. Supports zooming and panning but no out-of-box UI.
    Designed for streaming data points, used by Graphite monitoring system.

http://dygraphs.com/
    licensed under MIT, only for time series data.
    Chart isn't so pretty, supports zooming & panning but UI isn't so friendly.
    Designed for streaming data points.

http://square.github.io/cubism/
    Apache license. Special chart for time series data,  use much less vertical space.
    Designed for streaming data points.



http://syntagmatic.github.io/parallel-coordinates/
http://dc-js.github.io/dc.js/
http://square.github.com/crossfilter/
    Highly efficient exploration on large multi-dimensional dataset.



http://d3js.org/
    Low level drawing

http://nvd3.org
    Based on d3js, supports line/scatter/bubble/area/bar/pie/bullet chart.

http://raphaeljs.com/
    Low level drawing

http://g.raphaeljs.com/
    Based on RaphaelJs,  supports pie/bar/line/dot chart.
